Challenge

Your challenge is to identify a critical area of need and then conceive, design and build an experience that enables individual or group climate action that is accessible, achievable and measurable. Potential areas of focus could include energy use, food waste, diet change, re-use and recycling, transportation, and social and political activism.

Our Idea

Our mission was to develop a mobile app solution that mobilizes people to take action, creating a positive and meaningful impact in their community through connection and engagement. In working towards this goal, we created an app that can be linked to a smart trash can, and which enables an individual to monitor their carbon footprint by tracking their waste per day.

High Fidelity Prototype

We were expected to create minimum of seven screens over the weekend. After the ideation process, I took the lead on making the logo. I created the logo in Adobe Illustrator. I was also responsible to make the first five screens. The five screen included the visual tree shedding effect based on how much carbon imprint individual has done in a day. I designed the logo, the tree in Adobe Illustrator and color decision in the design. Our team used the real time Collabration feautre in Adobe XD to collborate the prototype.
